Solang Valley

About Solang Valley:

Solang Valley derives its name from a combination of words Solang (Nearby village) and Nallah (water stream). It is a side valley at the top of the Kullu Valley in Himachal Pradesh. Giant slopes of lawn comprise Solang Valley and provide its reputation as a popular ski resort. This valley is known for its summer and winter sport conditions. The sports most commonly offered are parachuting, paragliding and skating. Solang valley is also well known for the tea which is sold there.

Paragliding in Snow Mountains:

Have you ever dreamt of soaring through the sky or wondered what it’s like to fly like a bird? Paragliding in solang valley is the place. There are no words to portray the experience of the soundless wing flying high through the sky

Ropeway in Snow Mountains:

The best way to get a bird’s eye view of a snow mountains is to get onto a ropeway. The very feeling while you zip from one point to another with bated breath is no match to any other mode of conveyance.
