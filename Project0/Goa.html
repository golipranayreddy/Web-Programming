North Goa

North Goa is known for its beaches, which include Anjuna Beach, Candolim Beach, Mandrem Beach, Calangute Beach, Morjim Beach, and Arambol Beach. Other tourist sites include Fort Aguada, The church of Mae De Dues and the temple of Boghdeshwara. Chorao, Divar Island are islands of North Goa which are accessible via a ferry crossing.

Places to visit in North Goa :

  1. Forts
    • Chapora Fort
    • Aguada Fort
  2. Churches
    • Basilica of Bom Jesus
    • Se Cathedral
  3. Beaches
    • Baga Beach
    • Calangute Beach
    • Vagator Beach
    • Sinquerim Beach
  4. Mangueshi Temple
